Privacy Policy for Worst Extension Ever

Last Updated: March 11, 2026

1. Data Collection

The "Worst Extension Ever" does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal user data, browsing history, or sensitive information to any external servers. All extension settings (such as your chosen themes and layout preferences) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage.local API.

2. Use of Permissions

Our extension requests the following permissions for the reasons stated below:

  • activeTab: Used to identify the current website so that custom visual themes can be applied.
  • scripting: Used to inject CSS and JavaScript into the active page to perform visual transformations (e.g., the "Earthquake" effect and "Hotdog Stand" theme).
  • storage: Used to remember your chosen theme for specific websites so you don't have to re-select it every time you visit.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any information with third parties. There are no analytics, tracking pixels, or advertisements bundled with this extension.

4. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
